Swift Trek Travel - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Swift Trek Travel — This name suggests fast and adventurous travel experiences. It aligns with our mission of helping travelers explore efficiently and safely.

Site Purpose

This website provides destination guides, booking tools, and travel tips. It is a one-stop platform for planning trips, learning about locations, and getting travel-ready.

Scenarios

  • What are the top destinations to visit in Europe this summer?
  • Where can I find safe solo-travel tips for first-time travelers?

  • Accent Color: #F18F01 – Used for highlights and emphasized elements

Typography

  • Body Font: Open Sans – Used for general text content
